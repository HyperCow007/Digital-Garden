The Platinum King, The Final Arbiter, Warden of the Sky, Champion of the Mortal Race, The Last God

Bahamut is the final remanet of the divine age long since shattered, a god who did not fall when the heavens burned. Revered as the Platinum King (before the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]]), he once stood as the ultimate authority between mortals, a being whose judgement could end wars before they began, and whose presence alone enforced peace across the realm. 

From his seat in the floating city of [[The Mundane Plane/Luminaryx\|Luminaryx]], he watched over the world not as a ruler but as an arbiter, distant, impartial and absolute. 

During [[The Mundane Plane/History/Age of Accord/The Battle of Ashen Night\|The Battle of Ashen Night]], he used huge amounts of his power to empower his forces and summon the heroes, even bringing a hero back from death.

Drained of nearly all of his divine power, Bahamut entered a state of forced hibernation shortly after the battle. His once radiant form now lies dormant deep within [[The Mundane Plane/Luminaryx\|Luminaryx]], guarded without rest by the Dragon Lords. 

To the world, he is a fragile hope, he is the symbol the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]] choose. 

His continued existence is both a blessing and a burden. the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]], rally under the belief that as long as Bahamut lives the divine has not fully abandoned them, this is what the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Faith\|The Faith]] preaches.  

If the last god were to die, nothing will remain to stand against what comes next.

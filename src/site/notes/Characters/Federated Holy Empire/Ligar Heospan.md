The Grand Master of [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Imperial Military\|The Imperial Military]], Father of Legends, The Architect 

Ligar stands as one of the last living architects of [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Legend Units\|The Legend Units]]. As the Grand Master of [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Imperial Military\|The Imperial Military]], he commands the diverse and disciplined armies of the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]]. Though age has drawn him away from the frontlines. At 93 years old, Ligar is a relic of a different time. His body is old but his mind remains razor-sharp. 

In his youth Ligar was not merely a commander, but a mage of considerable power. During [[The Mundane Plane/History/Age of Accord/The Battle of Ashen Night\|The Battle of Ashen Night]] he was among the elite of [[Characters/Gods/Bahamut\|Bahamut]] responsible for summoning hero's from another world. 

Years following the war, he became the leading mind behind many of the Empire's initiative such as:
[[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Legend Units\|The Legend Units]] program.

# House Rules 
***List may be updated as Campaigns continues*** 

- **Meta-combat** - It's encouraged to use your turn to shout ideas to other characters (in game). Otherwise, keep meta strategy to a minimum, both for time savings and immersion's sake.
- Roll for hits and damage on your turn (**No Pre-Rolling**)
- **Rule of Cool** - It always wins, (unless its super game breaking)
- No Resurrection Spells 
- **Campfire Stories** - During long rests, a character may share a short story in character, whether from their past, a cultural tale, or a fictional story, helping build character, reveal lore, and potentially inspire in-game effects, while also giving the DM a chance to introduce past events and expand world lore, with NPCs stepping in if need be. 


# Down Time
Feat Re-training (Hours will be assigned)
- DC Check:
  - Success - Pass the time spent normally
  - Crit Success - Time Spent counts as Double 
  - Fail - Time counts as half 
  - Crit Fail - Time counts as wasted 

# Things to Think about before Session
- Think about why you were sentenced, (guilty, framed, political. accident?) if its something unique let me know
- Did you accept your fate immanently or did the Empire make you?
- Why hasn’t your character given up yet?

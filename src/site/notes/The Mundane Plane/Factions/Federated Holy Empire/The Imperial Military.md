The Military is a very diverse collection of regiments. As each region throughout the realm has its own regiment who are trained to fight within that climate or terrain. From this standard gear, battle tactics, and discipline differ from regiment to regiment. 

A regiment is typically made up of a handful of companies, each companies may consist of 2000 to 3000 troops. This is further broken down for organisation and logistics. Each regiment is typically named after the noble family associated with that region. The highest ranking officer of the Military is referred to as the Grandmaster. The Current Grandmaster is [[Characters/Federated Holy Empire/Ligar Heospan\|Ligar Heospan]]. 

[[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Faith\|The Faith]] and The Military are known to disagree and but heads with each other, over a many difference of opinions and the way the way they handle affairs. The military often has a pragmatic view on things seeing lost artifacts and technology as something that should be studied. 

The military also control the deploys the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/The Legend Units\|The Legend Units]] putting them out on the frontline or sending them on missions to fatal for regular troops.

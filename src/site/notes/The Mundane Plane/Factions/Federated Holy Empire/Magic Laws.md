The practice of magic within the realm is bound by strict legal doctrine. The following acts are expressly forbidden:

- **Unlicensed Necromancy**: 
  The raising, binding, or manipulation of the dead is prohibited under all circumstances.
- **Magical Compulsion**:
  The use of magic to control, influence, or override the will of another sentient being is illegal.
- **Enchanted Deception of Goods**: 
  Altering, disguising, or falsely enhancing items through magic for the purpose of deception or fraud is forbidden.
- **Artificial Creation of Minerals and Ores**: 
  The conjuration or transmutation of valuable natural resources is outlawed to preserve economic stability and prevent arcane exploitation.
- **Unlicensed Possession of Illegal Artifacts**: 
  Any relic tied to forbidden magic or past calamities must be surrendered to the Empire.
- **Unlicensed Scrying Prohibition**: 
  The use of divination magic to observe, locate, or gather information on individuals, locations, or events without imperial sanction is strictly forbidden.
- **Magical Experimentation Oversight**: 
  Arcane research must be approved and monitored. Unregulated experimentation especially on living beings is criminal.

Violation of these statutes is punishable by severe penalties, including imprisonment, magical restriction, or execution depending on severity and intent.

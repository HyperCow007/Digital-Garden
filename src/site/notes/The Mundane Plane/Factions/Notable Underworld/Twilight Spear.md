The Twilight Spear is not just a fleet, its a sovereign force that exist between nations and beyond law. Where most fleets sail for plunder, the Twilight Spear sails for purpose, bound to the will of [[Characters/Merirosvo\|Merirosvo]]. 

Formed from the after match of the [[The Mundane Plane/History/Age of Shattering/Age of Shattering\|Age of Shattering]]. To join the fleet is to abandon ones former life entirely swearing the *Oath of the Spear*:

***Oath of the Spear***
No Crown above us, no chain upon us, the sea is our kingdom, and the Spear our Law.

Though feared as pirates, they are more deliberate than the common rabble, striking precision blows against the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]]. 

At its core, the Twilight Spear is a tightly structured yet volatile fleet ruled absolutely by [[Characters/Merirosvo\|Merirosvo]], known only as the Sovereign, whose authority binds a collection of powerful captains  each commanding their own ship alongside his eerie enforcers, the Night Spears, and the broader crews, who follow him out of loyalty and shared purpose rather than fear. At the centre of it all sails the **_Eclipse Sovereign_**, a flagship steeped in lost elven magic, its self-mending hull and ever-intact sails marking it as something unnatural, which drifts through unnatural fog, defies navigation, and carries whispers of dead sailors still walking its decks, leaving many to question whether the Twilight Spear is cursed or blessed.

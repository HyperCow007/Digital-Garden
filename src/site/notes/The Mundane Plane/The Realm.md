# Vaeloris
Vaeloris is the largest continent within the world, hosting a variety of differing landscapes and climates from the cold snowy north, to the tempered heart and the hot desert sands on the south. All under the banner of the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]].

Long Winters, Short Summers on the northern end of the world. This region is not completely held by the *Federated Holy Empire*.

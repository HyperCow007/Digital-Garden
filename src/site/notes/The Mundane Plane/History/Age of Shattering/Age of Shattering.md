To the humans its a age of beginning, within elven records its regarded as a near extinction event. 

The conflicted persisted for four millennia. During this time, the mortal races alongside the remaining active deities, engaged in sustained warfare against entities originating from Hell and multiple outer planes. 

Documented consequences
- Elven populations experienced repeated generational losses, the several highborn bloodlines became extinct and the loss of traditional territories. 
- Dwarven strongholds and forges were abandoned or destroyed, resulting in a mass loss in technology 
- Orc war clans suffered high attrition rates, often resulting in total clan loss

Analysis of the conflict indicates that primary disadvantaged faced by the elder race was not military power but reproductive limitations. 

By the latter stages of the age, multiple sources indicate that several races approached extinction or were lost forever.

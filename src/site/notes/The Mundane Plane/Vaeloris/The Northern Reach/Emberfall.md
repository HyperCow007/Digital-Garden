Within [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Northern Reach/The Northern Reach\|The Northern Reach]] of the [[The Mundane Plane/The Realm\|The Realm]] 
Ruled by [[Characters/Gods/Tiamat\|Tiamat]]

When Tiamat reshaped the land, she did not mimic nature. She imposed draconic will upon it. The mountain rises not like a geological feature, but more like a crown. (23rd Year of the The Age of Accord)

Climate; warmer than surrounding northern regions due to the geothermal activity, ashfall is lighter than natural eruption (controlled venting), frequent thermal updrafts, dry heat near mountains, cooler coastal winds.

Geography; central volcano known as The Fivefold Crown, radial lava channels, obsidian plains, coastal black sand beaches, jagged basalt cliffs, extensive interior cavern network, natural mountain ring around the region forming a defensive basin.

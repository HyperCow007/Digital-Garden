Within [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Northern Reach/The Northern Reach\|The Northern Reach]] 
The jagged western coastline, the Azure Reach is a cold maritime power

Climate; cold ocean currents from the north, stormy springs and autumn., heavy coastal rainfall, inland rolling hills and temperate forests.  

Geography; natural harbours, numerous cliff-lined coast hosting fortresses, offshore islands holding naval watch posts, river deltas from Frostborne melt water.


Notes:
- Dominates northern sea trades, acts as a “gateway” between Storm Reach and Frostborne

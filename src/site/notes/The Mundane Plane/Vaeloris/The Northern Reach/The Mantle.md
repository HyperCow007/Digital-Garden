Geography; major east-west trade route, mountain spines dividing the north from deeper south, The Mantle is the stress sink of the northern region due to pressure from, glacial mass in the north and the artificially created Ember Fall, as a result low grade seismic activity is present. 

  
Notes: agriculturally vital, the land north south land bases crossing point, not fully controlled

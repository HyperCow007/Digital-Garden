Within [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Northern Reach/The Northern Reach\|The Northern Reach]] 

Climate; warmer than Frostborne but volatile, ocean driven storm systems slam into it first, frequent lighting storms, high winds year-round.

Geography; sharp coastal cliffs, inland highlands and broken plateaus. 

Notes: 

- A natural barrier between the western seas and the interior
- Land where storms build before rolling into the northern regions 
- Occasionally Ember Fall produces heat currents, that temperatures clash could intensify Storm Reach weather.

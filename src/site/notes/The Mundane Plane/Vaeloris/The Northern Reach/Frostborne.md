Within [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Northern Reach/The Northern Reach\|The Northern Reach]]

Being the uppermost region, Frostborne naturally becomes the true most northern mortal controlled expanse.

Climate; subarctic to polar, long winters, sea ice along coasts, inland permafrost and glacier carved valleys 

Geography; ice split mountains ridges, glacial lakes, boreal forests thinning to tundra

Notes:  
- Because the Azure Reach sits directly south, Frostborn feeds meltwater into Azure via major rivers  
- Northernmost frontline territory

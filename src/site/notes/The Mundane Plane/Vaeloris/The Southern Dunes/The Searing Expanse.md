This regions is the largest in [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Southern Dunes/The Southern Dunes\|The Southern Dunes]], and is mostly uninhabited due to the extreme heat conditions. 

Remanets of the old Southern Dune tribes live within it, groups of people who haven't conformed to the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]]. These people are known as the Vidrohi (*Vid-draw-he*).

The inhabited regions of the Southern Dunes are largely coastal due to the fact that, [[The Mundane Plane/Vaeloris/The Southern Dunes/The Searing Expanse\|The Searing Expanse]] is to hot to have permanent settlements, according the [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]] records.

The Southern dunes, has traditionally been a matriarchal society, it has retained this hierarchy even after becoming a part of  [[The Mundane Plane/Factions/Federated Holy Empire/Federated Holy Empire\|Federated Holy Empire]]. 

Their traditional culture makes them well suited to having adaptable people, and produce fine warrior, and sailors.
